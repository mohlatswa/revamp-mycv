Is Revamp MyCV really free?

Yes! The free plan gives you access to 16 professional templates, PDF downloads, and built-in ATS scoring. No credit card required. Pro (R49/month) and Premium (R149/month) plans unlock additional profession-specific templates and advanced features.

What is an ATS-friendly CV and why does it matter?

ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software that recruiters and large employers use to scan and filter CVs before a human ever sees them. Over 75% of South African corporate recruiters use ATS software.

An ATS-friendly CV uses clean formatting, standard section headings, and relevant keywords so the system can correctly parse your information. All Revamp MyCV templates are designed and tested to pass ATS scans successfully.

Can I use this for government job applications in South Africa?

Absolutely. We offer SA Formal and Government templates that follow the standard format expected by public service departments. These include all required fields like disability status (per the Employment Equity Act), language proficiency, ID details, and reference sections.

How do I download my CV as a PDF?

Fill in your details using the step-by-step wizard, choose a template on the Preview step, and click "Download PDF". Your CV is generated instantly in your browser — no waiting, no email required.

Is my data safe and private?

Your CV data is stored securely using Supabase cloud storage with encryption. We never share, sell, or use your personal information for marketing. You can export or permanently delete your data at any time from the Settings panel.

I have no work experience. Can I still make a CV?

Yes! Our Graduate and Career Change templates are designed specifically for people with little or no work experience. They emphasise education, skills, and potential rather than job history.

Can I save multiple CVs for different jobs?

Yes. Free users can save CVs, and Pro/Premium users get expanded limits (10 CVs for Pro, unlimited for Premium). Each saved CV remembers its template, colour scheme, and all your data.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation needed. Revamp MyCV runs entirely in your web browser on any device — phone, tablet, or computer. It also works as a Progressive Web App (PWA), so you can add it to your home screen for quick access.
